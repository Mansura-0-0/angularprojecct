About My Perfume App

Since I was young, I have always been very sensitive to smells. Even the smallest scent or unpleasant odor can affect me, but I also see this sensitivity as a blessing. It helps me truly appreciate beautiful smells and connect with them in a deeper way. For example, when I eat something like jackfruit-flavoured mochi, the scent helps me connect with the taste and enjoy it even more.

Since I started working in a pharmacy as a pharmacist assistant, I noticed something about myself. Whenever I get the chance to work at the perfume counter, I enjoy it much more than counting tablets or helping with injections. I love exploring perfumes, trying new scents, and discovering which ones suit me best.

This passion is what inspired me to create this app. I want to share my love for perfumes with others and help people find their perfect scent. I enjoy talking about perfumes, hearing about people’s favourite fragrances, smelling beautiful perfumes, and trying to guess the notes and ingredients behind them. It has become one of my favourite hobbies, and through this app, I hope to help others enjoy the world of perfume as much as I do.
